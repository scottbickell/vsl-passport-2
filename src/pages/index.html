DESTINATION Literacy.

Give your students the tools they need to take the journey of a lifetime.

Updated Voyager Passport is the K–5 reading intervention that provides struggling readers with explicit instruction, corrective feedback, and more time on task to master critical reading skills—in just 30 minutes per day.

Based on decades of research, Updated Voyager Passport is the reading intervention with the highest ESSA Rating: Tier 1 Strong . The updated program includes:

  • Systematic instruction in the five essential components of reading
  • Language/grammar instruction to support comprehension
  • Instruction of phonological awareness by skill progression
  • New Writing in Response to Reading activities with additional content, included in the practice component of each Adventure’s Optional Writing Projects
  • Up to 14 lessons per Adventure
  • Formative Assessments or Checkpoints (at Lessons 5 and 10) with targeted, direct instruction for differentiation specific to data
